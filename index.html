Course Goals

To develop statistical thinking skills to apply in your field of study and everyday life.

Course Catalog Description

Credit not allowed if student has prior credit for another ST course or BUS 350
Examining relationships between two variables using graphical techniques, simple linear regression and correlation methods. Producing data using experiment design and sampling. Elementary probability and the basic notions of statistical inference including confidence interval estimation and tests of hypothesis. One and two sample t-tests, one-way analysis of variance, inference for count data and regression.
